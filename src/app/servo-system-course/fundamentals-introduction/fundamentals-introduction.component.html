Introduction

Fundamentals module will help In understanding the fundamentals topic needed for servo system course. It is a module having chapters from basic engineering knowledge. These topics are essential for the main content which deals with sizing the servo motors. There is a chance that you may already be familiar with these topics. We have a pretest to know how much you already know, based on the result of which some topics of Fundamentals will be marked as complete which you may skip reading.

Pretest

The pretest is intended to judge your pre-requites knowledge for Servo System Training course. It is recommended to complete the test. Based on the pretest result Some topics of Fundamentals chapter will be marked as completed which you may skip reading. These topics will also be added to your module progress.
